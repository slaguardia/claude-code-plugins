# DMCA/Copyright Policy Audit

Audit DMCA policy for compliance with 17 U.S.C. ยง 512.

## Evaluation Criteria

### 1. DMCA Compliance Requirements

Check for all legally required elements:
- Designated agent contact information
- Proper takedown notice requirements
- Counter-notification requirements
- Procedure for restoring content
- Repeat infringer policy
- Warning about Section 512(f) penalties for false claims

Identify any missing or incomplete statutory elements.

### 2. Accuracy and Clarity of Procedures

Assess whether:
- Takedown process is described clearly and in correct sequence
- Steps for filing counter-notice are accurate and properly formatted
- Timelines (e.g., 10-14 business days) are compliant and clearly stated
- User would understand how to comply without legal expertise

### 3. Consistency With Other Policies

Check for:
- Alignment with ToS intellectual property sections
- No contradictions with Community Guidelines or Privacy Policy
- DMCA agent's email matches what is referenced elsewhere

### 4. Accessibility & Readability

Evaluate:
- Whether policy is understandable to non-lawyers
- If legal jargon is necessary or could be clarified
- Whether instructions are easy to follow with lists/bullets

### 5. Risk Assessment

Provide ratings (Low/Medium/High):
- DMCA Compliance Accuracy
- Clarity & Accessibility
- Cross-Policy Consistency

### 6. Recommendations

- Add missing statutory disclosures if any
- Improve clarity around timelines or steps
- Ensure designated agent information matches U.S. Copyright Office filing
- Add clarifying language about repeat infringer thresholds
- Improve structure or readability
